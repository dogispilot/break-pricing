About SkunkSniper.com

SkunkSniper.com was created in 2025 as an experimental project aimed at making sports card breaks more transparent.

Why does this exist?

For collectors: Ensure you have a fair shot in breaks and quickly find the most valuable cards in any set. A simple checklist isn't enough—you need complete and clear information.

For breakers: Price your sets with ease and provide full transparency to buyers. Quickly look up retail case prices and ensure your pricing is fair and accurate.

Are other sports coming?

Yes, we plan to expand beyond hockey. However, there’s no set timeline for adding other sports.

Are you affiliated with anyone?

No, SkunkSniper.com is an independent project with no affiliations. We don’t generate revenue, and all links provided are purely for the benefit of the card collecting community—there is no affiliate income.

Support the project

This is a self-funded initiative. If you’d like to support a cause we believe in, please consider donating to the UCSF Benioff Children's Hospital.

Where do you get the data?

Data is sourced directly from Upper Deck and Break Ninja. We thank them both very much.
