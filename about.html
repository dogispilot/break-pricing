About BreakPricing.com

BreakPricing.com was created in 2025 as an experimental project aimed at making sports card breaks more transparent and accessible.

Why does this exist?

For collectors: Ensure you have a fair shot in breaks and quickly find the most valuable cards in any set. A simple checklist isn't enough—you need complete and clear information.

For breakers: Price your sets with ease and provide full transparency to buyers. Quickly look up retail case prices and ensure your pricing is accurate.

Are other sports coming?

Yes, we plan to expand beyond hockey. However, there’s no set timeline for adding other sports.

Are you affiliated with anyone?

No, BreakPricing.com is an independent project with no affiliations. We don’t generate revenue, and all links provided are purely for the benefit of the community—there is no affiliate income.

Support the project
